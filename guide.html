Levels 1-76 Optimization Path

Core Philosophy

Focus on: Stamina ‚Üí Movement Speed ‚Üí Survivability ‚Üí Looting Speed

Avoid: Sound/noise perks (minimal impact)

PHASE 1: Early Survival

Levels 1-3

Goal: Stay alive and craft essentials

Level 1: Agile Croucher 1/5 - Foundation for mobility
Level 2: Revitalizing Squat 1/5 - Foundation for stamina
Level 3: In-Round Crafting 1/1 - PRIORITY - Craft bandages and shield rechargers

PHASE 2: Stamina Foundation

Levels 4-14

Goal: Move faster across the map

Level 4: Nimble Climber 1/5 - Faster climbing
Levels 5-9: Marathon Runner 5/5 - Max stamina pool
Levels 10-14: Youthful Lungs 5/5 - Max stamina regen
What you get: Significantly more stamina for running and better regeneration

PHASE 3: Heavy Armor Support

Levels 15-19

Goal: Use medium shields without movement penalty

Levels 15-19: Used to the Weight 5/5 - Maintain speed with better shields

PHASE 4: Combat Stamina

Levels 20-25

Goal: Survive ambushes with emergency stamina

Level 20: Blast-Born 1/5 - Unlock path to Fight or Flight
Levels 21-25: Fight or Flight 5/5 - Emergency stamina when shield breaks

PHASE 5: Breach Speed & Emergency Tools

Levels 26-33

Goal: Open containers faster, unlock survival mechanics

Level 26: Gentle Pressure 1/5 - Unlock Proficient Pryer
Levels 27-29: Proficient Pryer 3/5 - Faster breaching (partial)
Level 30: Unburdened Roll 1/1 - FREE stamina after dodge rolling
Level 31: Survivor's Stamina 1/1 - Stamina regen when critically hurt
Levels 32-33: Proficient Pryer 5/5 - Max breach speed
What you unlock: Two critical emergency mechanics for when things go wrong

PHASE 6: Mobility Completion

Levels 34-39

Goal: Maximize stamina efficiency during movement

Levels 34-36: Slip and Slide 3/5 - Longer slides (author's preference) OR Sturdy Ankles
Level 37: Sturdy Ankles 1/5 - Minimal fall damage
Level 38: Carry the Momentum 1/1 - Better stamina usage while moving
Level 39: Calming Stroll 1/1 - Stamina efficiency boost
Alternative: You can swap Slip and Slide/Sturdy Ankles priorities based on playstyle

PHASE 7: Looting Efficiency

Levels 40-50

Goal: Loot faster = more XP per round

Levels 40-43: Revitalizing Squat 5/5 - Max stamina from squatting
Levels 44-48: Looter's Instincts 5/5 - Faster looting speed
Levels 49-50: Agile Croucher 3/5 - Faster crouching (partial)

PHASE 8: Carry Capacity Setup

Levels 51-59

Goal: Carry more loot without being slowed

Level 51: Silent Scavenger 1/5 - Unlock path (not for noise benefit)
Level 52: Good as New 1/1 - Quality of life
Level 53: Traveling Tinkerer 1/1 - Craft raider hatch keys (lifesaver)
Level 54: Suffer in Silence 1/1 - Unlock Broad Shoulders
Levels 55-59: Broad Shoulders 5/5 - Rarely become over-encumbered

PHASE 9: Speed Looting

Levels 60-64

Goal: Maximum looting efficiency

Levels 60-64: Looter's Luck 5/5 - Fastest possible looting

PHASE 10: Final Push to Breaches

Levels 65-73

Goal: Handle heavy loads and unlock breach access

Levels 65-69: Stubborn Mule 5/5 - Stamina regen when over-encumbered
Levels 70-71: Agile Croucher 5/5 - Max crouch speed
Level 72: Three Deep Breaths 1/5 - Extra stamina boost
Level 73: Security Breach 1/1 - UNLOCK BREACHES

PHASE 11: Final Optimization

Levels 74-76

Goal: Fine-tune carrying capacity and weapon handling

Level 74: A Little Extra 1/1 - Extra resources for crafting heals
Level 75: Loaded Arms 1/1 - Weapons weigh less = carry more
Level 76: Crawl Before You Walk 1/5 - Crawl faster to safety when downed
