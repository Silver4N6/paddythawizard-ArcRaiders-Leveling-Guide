# Arc Raiders Leveling Guide

*Based on the guide by u/paddythawizard*

---

## Core Philosophy

**Focus on:** Stamina → Movement Speed → Survivability → Looting Speed

**Avoid:** Sound/noise perks (minimal impact)

---

## Phase 1: Early Survival (Levels 1-3)

**Goal:** Stay alive and craft essentials

| Level | Perk | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| 1 | Agile Croucher 1/5 | Foundation for mobility |
| 2 | Revitalizing Squat 1/5 | Foundation for stamina |
| 3 | In-Round Crafting 1/1 | **PRIORITY** — Craft bandages and shield rechargers |

---

## Phase 2: Stamina Foundation (Levels 4-14)

**Goal:** Move faster across the map

| Level | Perk | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| 4 | Nimble Climber 1/5 | Faster climbing |
| 5-9 | Marathon Runner 5/5 | Max stamina pool |
| 10-14 | Youthful Lungs 5/5 | Max stamina regen |

**Result:** Significantly more stamina for running and better regeneration

---

## Phase 3: Heavy Armor Support (Levels 15-19)

**Goal:** Use medium shields without movement penalty

| Level | Perk | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| 15-19 | Used to the Weight 5/5 | Maintain speed with better shields |

---

## Phase 4: Combat Stamina (Levels 20-25)

**Goal:** Survive ambushes with emergency stamina

| Level | Perk | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| 20 | Blast-Born 1/5 | Unlock path to Fight or Flight |
| 21-25 | Fight or Flight 5/5 | Emergency stamina when shield breaks |

---

## Phase 5: Breach Speed & Emergency Tools (Levels 26-33)

**Goal:** Open containers faster, unlock survival mechanics

| Level | Perk | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| 26 | Gentle Pressure 1/5 | Unlock Proficient Pryer |
| 27-29 | Proficient Pryer 3/5 | Faster breaching (partial) |
| 30 | Unburdened Roll 1/1 | Free stamina after dodge rolling |
| 31 | Survivor's Stamina 1/1 | Stamina regen when critically hurt |
| 32-33 | Proficient Pryer 5/5 | Max breach speed |

**Unlocks:** Two critical emergency mechanics for when things go wrong

---

## Phase 6: Mobility Completion (Levels 34-39)

**Goal:** Maximize stamina efficiency during movement

| Level | Perk | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| 34-36 | Slip and Slide 3/5 | Longer slides (author's preference) *OR* Sturdy Ankles |
| 37 | Sturdy Ankles 1/5 | Minimal fall damage |
| 38 | Carry the Momentum 1/1 | Better stamina usage while moving |
| 39 | Calming Stroll 1/1 | Stamina efficiency boost |

> **Note:** Swap Slip and Slide/Sturdy Ankles priorities based on your playstyle

---

## Phase 7: Looting Efficiency (Levels 40-50)

**Goal:** Loot faster = more XP per round

| Level | Perk | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| 40-43 | Revitalizing Squat 5/5 | Max stamina from squatting |
| 44-48 | Looter's Instincts 5/5 | Faster looting speed |
| 49-50 | Agile Croucher 3/5 | Faster crouching (partial) |

---

## Phase 8: Carry Capacity Setup (Levels 51-59)

**Goal:** Carry more loot without being slowed

| Level | Perk | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| 51 | Silent Scavenger 1/5 | Unlock path (not for noise benefit) |
| 52 | Good as New 1/1 | Quality of life |
| 53 | Traveling Tinkerer 1/1 | Craft raider hatch keys (lifesaver) |
| 54 | Suffer in Silence 1/1 | Unlock Broad Shoulders |
| 55-59 | Broad Shoulders 5/5 | Rarely become over-encumbered |

---

## Phase 9: Speed Looting (Levels 60-64)

**Goal:** Maximum looting efficiency

| Level | Perk | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| 60-64 | Looter's Luck 5/5 | Fastest possible looting |

---

## Phase 10: Final Push to Breaches (Levels 65-73)

**Goal:** Handle heavy loads and unlock breach access

| Level | Perk | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| 65-69 | Stubborn Mule 5/5 | Stamina regen when over-encumbered |
| 70-71 | Agile Croucher 5/5 | Max crouch speed |
| 72 | Three Deep Breaths 1/5 | Extra stamina boost |
| 73 | Security Breach 1/1 | **UNLOCK BREACHES** |

---

## Phase 11: Final Optimization (Levels 74-76)

**Goal:** Fine-tune carrying capacity and weapon handling

| Level | Perk | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| 74 | A Little Extra 1/1 | Extra resources for crafting heals |
| 75 | Loaded Arms 1/1 | Weapons weigh less = carry more |
| 76 | Crawl Before You Walk 1/5 | Crawl faster to safety when downed |

### Level 76 Alternatives

- **Effortless Swing 1/5** — One extra melee swing (PvP/Harvester)
- **Downed But Determined 1/5** — Slightly longer bleedout time
- **Crawl Before You Walk 1/5** — Author's choice for safety
